More About Us

More About Us (First-person paragraph): I'm yasha mushtaq a fashion artist who loves what I do and the stories I
tell. My experiences in fashion have influenced my approach to design - not only as garments, but also as a
means of communication. I am committed to sustainability; I want to design and produce pieces that are
conscientious, considerate, and long-lasting. I combine contemporary styles and attention to detail, striving to
merge artistry with functionality. My designs aim to create a better future for fashion while remaining true to
my style.
